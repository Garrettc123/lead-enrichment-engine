# Lead Enrichment Engine - Sales Playbook

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

### Primary: B2B Sales Teams
- Company size: 50-500 employees
- Sales team: 10-50 SDRs
- Revenue: $10M-$100M ARR
- Current tools: Salesforce, Outreach, Apollo
- Pain: Poor lead data quality
- Budget: $5K-$20K/year for sales tools

### Secondary: Marketing Agencies
- Agency size: 10-50 employees
- Clients: B2B SaaS, tech companies
- Pain: Manual lead research
- Budget: $2K-$10K/year
